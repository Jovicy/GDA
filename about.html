About

The AFRIKA GLOBAL GROUP (AG GROUP) LLC is a US-AFRICA based entrepreneurship and financial inclusion promotion, technology & business development and media company, founded and managed by competent and committed Africans, African-Americans and Non-Africans entrepreneurs & investors for the economic and social development in the USA and Africa. This is a citizen company fulfilling its commitments and promoting the creation of economic and social wealth, the creation of productive employment, environmental protection and promotion of human rights & peace worldwide

Four strategic principles guiding our thinking and our actions everyday include:

  • The creation
  • The enhancement of resources
  • The Integration
  • The synergy of actors.

    • Our development methodology is based on the constant search for business governance which globally consists of:

  • Know and identify
  • Communicate and act togethe
  • Produce and create new capabilities of wealth.

    • The AFRIKA GLOBAL GROUP (AG GROUP) LLC is also involved in the detection and development of a new generation of entrepreneurs and business leaders, especially women and young people, who can meet the challenges of the third millennium. Also, we provide international right partners and investors for all kind of economic and social projects. The AFRIKA GLOBAL GROUP (AG GROUP) LLC develops partnership and sustainable customer relations with different sectors:

  • Public Sector (governments, public agencies and enterprises, international organizations, local authorities ...)
  • Private Sector (private companies, professional associations, chambers of commerce ...)
  • Social Sector (associations, NGOs, foundations ...).
  • International Sector (UN System, bilateral Agencies...)

    • Our Mission

    The GROUPE DU DEVELOPPEMENT DE L’AFRIQUE (GDA) is a pan-African non-governmental organization for studies/research, technical assistance/advice, education/training, and communication/information in Gender/DSSR/GBV, Women's Entrepreneurship & Youth, Education, Health, Strategy, Governance, and Sustainable Development for a new Africa at peace with itself and the rest of the world.

    It is a true "Idea Box" (Think-Tank) and "Action Box" (Do-Tank) in the service of African Renaissance, with four strategic principles guiding its thinking and action every day, namely:

  • Creation
  • Resource enhancement
  • Integration
  • Actor synergy.

    • Our development method is based on the continuous search for development governance which consists of:

  • Knowing and identifying
  • Communicating and acting together
  • Producing and creating new wealth capacities.

    • For the realization of its activities, GDA develops customer relations and sustainable partnerships with different sectors: Public sector (State, administrations and public enterprises, local authorities, etc.) Private sector (private companies, professional associations, chambers of commerce, etc.) Civil sector (associations, NGOs, etc.) International sector (UN, AU, EU, bilateral cooperation, etc.).

    The GROUPE DU DEVELOPPEMENT DE L’AFRIQUE (GDA) is also committed to identifying and developing a new generation of economic, social, and cultural entrepreneurs capable of meeting the major challenges of the third millennium in Africa. Also, it works to inspire the creation of local and national economic institutions capable of boosting the creation of lasting wealth and decent jobs in Africa.
